Our Story

Built on rallies,
rooted in community.

Nestled in the heart of La Lucia, Durban, our club is the meeting point for players of every level — from first-timers picking up a racket for the first time to seasoned competitors chasing every point.

We believe tennis is better together. Whether you're here for a social hit on a Wednesday afternoon, a competitive league match, or simply a catch-up after a hard-fought set — La Lucia Tennis Club is your home court.

With floodlit courts, a welcoming clubhouse, social sessions running throughout the week, and a calendar packed with events, there's always a reason to come back.

What We Offer

World-class facilities,
local soul.

Floodlit Courts

Play day or night on our well-maintained courts — suitable for social hits, coaching sessions, and competitive league play.

Clubhouse

Relax and socialise in our comfortable clubhouse — the perfect post-match venue to connect with fellow members.

Social Events

Round-robins, club nights, sundowners, and year-end galas — the social calendar keeps the off-court camaraderie alive all year.

Leagues

Compete in organised club leagues for juniors, ladies, and men. Included with adult and junior membership — test your game against the best in the area.

Easy Access

Located at 44 Oakleigh Avenue, La Lucia, with ample parking and a welcoming environment for players and families alike.

Junior Programme

Nurturing the next generation. Our junior programme, supported by Roscoe's Academy, develops skills, sportsmanship, and a lifelong love of tennis.
